Cleveland Cavaliers

As a Cleveland Cavalier Lebron joinned this team as just a 18 year old kid out of highschool

His accomplishments include three NBA championships, four NBA Most Valuable Player Awards, three NBA Finals MVP Awards, and two Olympic gold medals. James has appeared in fifteen NBA All-Star Games and been named NBA All-Star MVP three times.

Cleveland Cavalier Accolades/Statistics
  • Played 11 Seasons with the Cavs
  • Averaged 27.2 Points per game
  • Averaged 7.3 Assists per game
  • Averaged 7.3 Rebounds per game
  • Averaged 1.6 Steals per game
  • Won an NBA Championship in 2016 and was Named Finals MVP
